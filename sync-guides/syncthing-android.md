# Syncthing Android

Use Syncthing-Fork or Syncthing Android alternatives. Open the synced folder as an Obsidian vault. Avoid syncing heavy raw sources to low-storage devices.

## Safety

- Do not expose Syncthing GUI publicly.
- Do not sync secrets or `.env` files.
- Write human notes into `_INBOX_USER/`.
- Let agents promote reviewed material into canon.

# Mobius Iphone

Use Möbius Sync with an Obsidian local vault. Prefer a lightweight mobile folder that excludes `raw/`. If Obsidian is empty while Möbius says up-to-date, they are pointing at different local folders.

## Safety

- Do not expose Syncthing GUI publicly.
- Do not sync secrets or `.env` files.
- Write human notes into `_INBOX_USER/`.
- Let agents promote reviewed material into canon.
